Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of the my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for delciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in bowl Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  3. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  4. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continuew to cook on the plate untile served)
